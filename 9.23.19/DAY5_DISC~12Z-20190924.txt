For the North Atlantic...Caribbean Sea and the Gulf of Mexico:

The National Hurricane Center is issuing advisories on Tropical
Storm Jerry, located a few hundred miles southwest of Bermuda, on
recently upgraded Tropical Storm Karen, located over the
northeastern Caribbean Sea, and on Tropical Storm Lorenzo, located
over the eastern tropical Atlantic.

southwestern Gulf of Mexico during the next few days before it
